## Summary

Track job applications automatically, detect supported job pages, and manage your pipeline without leaving the tab.

## Detailed Description

AppTrail helps job seekers capture opportunities the moment they find them and keep their search organized in one place.

With the AppTrail extension you can:

- detect supported job postings on LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, Ashby, and other career pages;
- save a job directly into your AppTrail pipeline from the side panel;
- track repeated visits to company career pages and convert that activity into pipeline entries;
- surface recruiter and alumni contact suggestions after saving a role; and
- sync extension activity back to your AppTrail account using a user-scoped API key.

AppTrail is designed for focused job-search workflow management, not broad browsing analytics. It only runs on supported career-related pages and only uses the permissions required for detection, sync, and the side-panel capture flow.
