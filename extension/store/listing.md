## Detailed Description

```text
AppTrail helps you keep your job search in one system instead of spreading it across tabs, notes, and spreadsheets.

The extension works alongside the AppTrail dashboard. When you open a supported job page, AppTrail can recognize the listing, open a side panel, and let you save the role directly into your pipeline.

What the extension does:

- Detects supported job pages across major applicant tracking systems and career sites
- Extracts job details such as title, company, location, and salary when available
- Opens a side panel so you can review and edit the captured information before saving
- Tracks repeated visits to company career pages so you can spot companies you are spending time on
- Detects common application submission pages on supported platforms
- Queues activity locally when you are offline and syncs it when the connection returns

The extension is built for focused job-search use. It does not try to monitor general browsing activity. It only runs on supported job and career-related pages that match its detection rules.

AppTrail works best when paired with the dashboard, which adds:

- a full application pipeline
- recruiter and contact tracking
- Gmail sync and hiring-email classification
- interview tracking
- resume parsing and tailoring
- job search and research workflows
- reporting, audits, and extraction feedback

Setup:

1. Sign in to AppTrail
2. Generate an API key in Settings
3. Paste that key into the extension setup screen
4. Start saving jobs from supported pages

Privacy:

- The extension uses a user-scoped API key
- Job data stays local until you save or sync it
- The extension does not sell personal data
- The extension is limited to supported job-related pages

Support:

- Product site: https://apptrail.com
- Privacy policy: https://apptrail.com/privacy
- Source code: https://github.com/ColbyRReichenbach/jobRadar
```
